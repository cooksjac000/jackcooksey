About Me

So in case you were wondering what this is all about ...

I'm a robotics engineer studying mechanical engineering at UT Austin. And this is my portfolio!

Ever since high school, I've been interested in all things robotic! Now that I'm in college, I'm developing my skills with professional, research, and extracurricular experience.

The Highlights

These are my most significant projects

Comparator

I founded and led a 3lb combat robotics team from January 2025 to the present day. We developed Comparator, a 4-wheel drive vertical spinner. In the Texas Robo Rumble, we placed 5th overall and 1st out of rookie teams.

ONA

I worked and studied in Barcelona, Spain, over the Summer of 2025. I worked at Vaive Logistics, which is developing a last-mile delivery robot, ONA. There, I created a communication pipeline that enabled sub-0.5s user operation on the ONA robot.

XLeRobot V2

I have been a research assistant at the Oden Institute since late August 2025. I am a member of the XLeRobot V2 team, where we are developing a cost-effective, capable household robot based on the XLeRobot. Currently, we are building the XLeRobot platform to develop and test AI models to autonomously complete household tasks.

9105 Technotalons

I was a founding member and 2-year leader of the First Robotics Competition team 9105 Technotalons from Fall 2022-Spring 2024. I led the team through the whole engineering process over a fast-paced 8-week build period to develop a competitive solution to an engineering challenge. In my two years as the leader, my team competed in two state championships and one world championship.
