Fall 2022-Spring 2024

9105 Technotalons Team Leader
First Robotics Competition

I was a founding member and team captain of the First Robotics Competition team 9105 Technotalons. There, I led a team of more than 30 kids in building competitive robots for yearly competitions. I mentored my team in all sorts of engineering skills, all the way from CAD and wiring design to assembling the robot and machining using CNC machines. Under my leadership, the team competed in the world championships one year and the state championships both years.

Problem

Every year, FIRST sends out a challenge to thousands of teams across the world to develop solutions in a 6-week build phase, after which we compete at regional, state, and world levels. For example, we might be challenged to develop a robot which can shoot balls into a goal for points.

Timeline

There is a 6-week build phase followed by 6 weeks of regional competition, with state and worlds after that.

Design Process

Each Spring, I led my team through the whole engineering process. After kickoff, we learned the rules inside and out while brainstorming ideas for different mechanisms on the robot. We then went through rapid prototyping to prove our ideas work before dedicating major resources to developing them.

Design Process

We then 3D printed and machined all the parts we would need to assemble our robot, and built the first version. Besides being the team captain, I was also head of CNC, so I CAMed and CNCed most of our parts, along with teaching my teammates how to use the machines. After that, it was on to programming and iterating our design before our first regional competition. Finally, we would get a lot of insight into our performance from the regional competition and would iterate on our programming and design up until the end of the competition season.

Results

Under my leadership, my team went to one world championship and two state championship competitions. I was also recognised as a Dean's List Semi-Finalist for exceptional leadership.

Skills

Leadership, Conflict Resolution, Teamwork, Mentorship, CAD (Onshape), CAM (Fusion 360), CNC(Router and 3-axis), Soldering, 3D Printing, Laser Cutting, Machine Shop
